How to Play

Goal

Win exactly the number of Stacks (tricks) you bid each round.

Setup

4 players, 10 cards each from a standard deck (12 left out).

Action moves clockwise. The player with the lowest-ranked card leads the first Stack.

Suit Ranking

Suits are ranked: ♣ Clubs > ♠ Spades > ♥ Hearts > ♦ Diamonds.

Any card of a higher suit beats any card of a lower suit.

Leading a Stack

The leader must play a ♦ Diamond if they have one.

Once other suits are introduced, the leader may play any unlocked suit.

If they have no unlocked suits, they play their lowest available suit.

Following

If you have a card of the same suit as the lead, you must play it.

Otherwise, play a lower-ranked suit. If you can't, play the next higher suit. Clubs is the last resort.

Winning a Stack

The highest-ranked card wins. Suit rank beats card rank —

a 2♣ beats a K♠, a 2♥ beats a K♦, and so on.

The winner leads the next Stack.

Bidding & Scoring

Before play, bid 0–10 Stacks you think you'll win.

Hit your bid: bid × 10 points.

Under bid: score 0 for the round.

Over bid: bid × 10 minus 10 for every Stack over.

Bid 0 and win 0: score 100 points!

Minimum round score is 0. Aces are high.

AI

AI players bid based on hand strength and play to meet their bid.
